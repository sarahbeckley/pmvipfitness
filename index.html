I'm Patrick Morales, founder of PM VIP Fitness, and I'm here to tell you that there is hope when struggling with depression or anxiety. I am an ACE certified personal trainer that has experienced severe depression and have used physical exercise, nutrition and mindset to manage this dehabilitating time in my life.


Exercise and proper nutrition has helped shape who I am today and gave me the confidence to move forward no matter what I am going through. I couldn't have accomplished this happier self without molding my mindset through meditation. Mindset has improved my life drastically and i've realized that this is the foundation to recovery.


My functional training program will help build the essentials needed to permanently change your life and help you better cope with the effects of depression or anxiety. I specialize in people working with depression or anxiety, but I also provide services for the more experienced weight lifter.
